# arboretum
An arboretum (latin plural: arboreta) is a botanical collection of
trees and shrubs; a living museum of trees and woody plants, 
grown and cared for by an organization committed to their stewardship.

### sequoia sempervirens
This species includes the tallest living trees on Earth, reaching up to 116.22 m and up to 8.9 m
in diameter.

The species is monoecious, with pollen and seed cones on the same plant.
